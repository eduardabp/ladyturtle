In 2014, I moved to Europe. Was my first time outside South America and everything amazed me. But most of all, was my first contact with good beer! In Brazil, we have a long history with extremely cold beer, mainly because of 2 reasons: it goes well with barbecue in days that reach 40�C degrees and the low temperature disguises the fact that they taste bad. Brands

Since 2005, the craft beer market has experienced a continuously growth and it is expected to double his valuation until 2032. Although Europe was a bit late on catching up with this new trend, we can see now the market becoming a part of local culture and tourism industry, and some brands already reached an industrial scale production. Here are a few brands that you can easily find on your local craft beer shop. If not, you can always shop online (most of these brands has their own web shop and ship to all Europe!).

1. To Ol

"At To Ol we do contemporary beers. We brew a wide range of potent, provocative, fresh and floral beers; hoppy, balanced and complex beers both light, dark, strong and simple. We brew the beers we want to drink ourselves and work to do the best beers in the world. We never compromise on taste and balance and keep pushing the boundaries of great beer-crafting."

Beer Festivals

It can be good to drink beer by yourself once in a while, but let's be honest: beer is better with friends! And there is no place better to hang out with your drink buddies ou make some new friends than beer festivals! I had some of the best memories of my life in events like this and some long friendships that started with sharing a beer in a festival.
